Account deletion that was created in Android applications developed by VIS Apps developer

This page provides information about account deletion

How to request account deletion

Please send an email requesting account deletion to developer email address visappshelp@gmail.com. In your email, be sure to include the email address you provided when creating your account. To confirm account deletion, we will send an email to the email address you provided when creating your account.

What data will be deleted after deleting your account

Once your request has been approved, we will immediately delete all data associated with your account without the possibility of recovery.
